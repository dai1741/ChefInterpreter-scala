Strong-tasting Bread.

Checking the instruments

Ingredients.
222 g sugar
55 g salt
1 tablespoon vinegar
100 ml soy sauce
10 tablespoons miso
999 g flour

Method.
Liquefy miso.
Put flour into the mixing bowl.
Put sugar into the mixing bowl.
Put salt into the mixing bowl.
Put vinegar into the mixing bowl.
Put soy sauce into the mixing bowl.
Mix well.
Pour contents of the mixing bowl into the baking dish.
Stir for 2 minutes.
Put miso into the mixing bowl.
Pour contents of the mixing bowl into the 2nd baking dish.
Clean mixing bowl.
Put vinegar into the mixing bowl.
Put vinegar into the mixing bowl.
Put vinegar into the mixing bowl.
Put salt into the mixing bowl.
Stir vinegar into the mixing bowl.
Put miso into the mixing bowl.
Pour contents of the mixing bowl into the 3rd baking dish.
Put sugar into the 2nd mixing bowl.
Put miso into the 2nd mixing bowl.
Pour contents of the 2nd mixing bowl into the 4th baking dish.

Serves 4.
