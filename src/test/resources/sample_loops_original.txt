Loops.

derp loop

Ingredients.
2 eggs
3 g butter
100 g salt
10 g sugar

Method.
Melt the butter.
Put eggs into mixing bowl.
Burn the butter.
Stir the mixing bowl for 5 minutes.
Remove eggs.
Melt the butter until burned.
Cook the sugar.
Add salt to mixing bowl.
Cook the sugar until cooked.
Melt the salt until melted.
Pour contents of the mixing bowl into the baking dish.

Serves 1.
