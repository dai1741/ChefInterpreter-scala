Regular ordinary Russian bread.

Checking arithmetic operations and something like that

Ingredients.
900 g flour
280 g sugar
10 g salt
1 tablespoon vinegar
100 ml soy sauce
miso

Method.
Put flour into mixing bowl.
Add sugar to mixing bowl.
Put flour into mixing bowl.
Remove salt from mixing bowl.
Add vinegar.
Put salt into mixing bowl.
Combine soy sauce.
Fold miso into mixing bowl.
Put miso into mixing bowl.
Divide sugar into 1st mixing bowl.
Pour contents of the mixing bowl into the baking dish.

Serves 1.
